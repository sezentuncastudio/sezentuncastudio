About the Studio

Design as a form
of thinking

Sezen Tunca Studio is an Istanbul-based creative studio founded in 2020. We build brand identities that are purposeful, precise, and quietly unforgettable.

Our practice spans brand strategy, visual identity, packaging design, and art direction — everywhere a brand shows up in the world to meaningfully connect.

Process

A thoughtful process that moves
at the pace of good ideas.

01
Introduction
Understanding the brand, the founder, the product and the intention behind it.
02
Brand Core
Defining what the brand believes in, how it behaves, and what it wants to be known for.
03
Visual World
Creating the moodboard, references, atmosphere and first visual direction.
04
Feedback & Refinement
Clarifying the direction through conversation, feedback and creative editing.
05
Identity Design
Designing the logo, typography, colour palette, visual language and key brand assets.
06
Brand System
Building a clear and usable system that can live across packaging, print, digital and social touchpoints.
07
Final Delivery
Preparing the brand files and guidelines so the identity can be used consistently and confidently.
